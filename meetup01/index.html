In this meetup, you'll be pitching your idea and forming your team, then start planning with them on how to build your project.

Your team will need to to figure out what tools and technologies will help and how to learn them. You'll create your own curriculum for the next 3 weeks, and we'll help you do it.

  • This meetup is free of cost.
  • Laptops are mandatory, charging ports will be available.
