In this meetup, you'll be collaborating with your team members to learn together and build your project. This will be done in the pair programming format.

In case you're not familiar, pair programming is a coding technique in which two people work together on a single system. One, the driver, writes code while the other, the navigator, reviews it. The two programmers switch roles frequently.

  • This meetup is free of cost.
  • Laptops are mandatory, charging ports will be available.
